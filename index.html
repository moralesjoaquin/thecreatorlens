Card-only laundromats earn 20–30% more than coin-only — add readers from day one

1
Research high-renter-density areas with limited competition within 1 mile

Contact existing laundromat owners directly — many want to sell but aren't publicly listed
3
Finance via SBA 7(a) loan — laundromats qualify easily with a good location
